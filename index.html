VISIT


Before you visit, please read the rules below, and check the FAQ page. Thank you!


While everyone is welcomed, we strongly recommend that if you do not visit us if you have any of the following:

  • Severe allergy to cats
  • Young children who may be disruptive
  • Any health concerns that the presence of cats might make worst

RULES

By entering, you agree to comply with all rules the Stray Cafe may have, and release us from any and all liablity

  • We only allow 6 people in the cat portion of our cafe
  • Do not feed the cats!
  • Please do not attempt to free the cats!
  • Cats are people, sometimes they like to love, play, and sometimes they want to hangout, sometimes they want to be left alone.
  • Please do not wake up or pick up the cats
  • Please keep the area clea, quiet, and relaxing
  • Please no flash photos
  • Please be mindful of others who may want their turn to play with cats
